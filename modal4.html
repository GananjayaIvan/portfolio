Steps to take & Considerations




1. Data Cleaning must be done to ensure that noise data and outliers doesn't affect the results.

2. Descriptive Analytics helps us find information about the data such as central tendency (mean, median, mode), dispersion (range, variance, standard deviation), and distribution (skewness, kurtosis). By examining these metrics, analysts can gain a deeper understanding of the dataset's structure and patterns.

3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), hypothesis testing, and deriving insights and recommendations are integral stages in the analytical process, each playing a crucial role in extracting meaningful information from data. EDA involves examining the dataset through various graphical and statistical methods to uncover patterns, anomalies, and relationships among variables. It helps analysts understand the data's distribution, outliers, and potential biases, providing a foundation for further investigation.

4. Hypothesis testing, on the other hand, is used to validate assumptions or theories about the data. It involves formulating a hypothesis, typically stating a null hypothesis (no effect or relationship) and an alternative hypothesis (some effect or relationship exists), and then using statistical tests to determine the likelihood of the observed results occurring by chance. This rigorous process helps confirm or refute hypotheses, providing statistical evidence to support decision-making.

5. Deriving insights and recommendations is the culmination of EDA and hypothesis testing. It involves interpreting the findings in the context of the problem or question at hand, drawing meaningful conclusions, and proposing actionable recommendations based on the data analysis. These recommendations could range from operational improvements to strategic decisions, depending on the insights uncovered during the analytical process.

Data Cleaning Process
In this stage, we need to make sure the data is clean by removing rows and columns missing and duplicate data, changing the data into appropiate type, imputing missing data, and adding new KPI for analysis.

No Action Reasoning
1 Remove rows and columns with missing data With only 54 entries out of 10160, the status column will be removed.
2 Remove rows with duplicated data By using transaction id, we can remove duplicated data.
3 Change data type of columns that is not suitable Changing the type of column for price, discount, and total revenue columns to Currency (IDR).
4 Impute Missing Data Using these formulas:
  • Revenue = (Price * Quantity) - Discount
  • Price = (Revenue + Discount) / Quantity
  • Quantity = (Revenue + Discount) / Price
  • Discount = (Price * Quantity) - Revenue
5 Adding new KPI for analysis We can calculate ROI based on: Revenue / Discount
